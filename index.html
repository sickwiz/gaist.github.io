Gamified Approach to Information Security Training (GAIST) is a project developed as part of the graduate project (B.Tech) in Computer Science and Engineering at Jaypee Institute of Information Technology (JIIT), Sector 62, Noida, India - 201307, by Aamir Ahmad and Vivek Dwivedi under the supervision of Dr. P. Raghu Vamsi, Assistant Professor, Dept of CSE. This project aims to provide hands-on training to security professionals and students through the simulation of OWASP top 10 vulnerabilities.
